About Us

About IEEE

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE and its members inspire a global community to innovate for a better tomorrow through its more than 400,000 members in over 160 countries, and its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities.



About IEEE SSIT SB

The IEEE Student Branch at Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology, Tumkur, was established to foster technical innovation and provide students with a community to explore their interests in engineering and technology. We organize numerous events, workshops, and competitions to bridge the gap between academia and industry.



Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide a platform for SSIT students to develop their professional and technical skills, to network with peers and industry experts, and to contribute to the IEEE community.

Vision: To be a leading student branch recognized for its technical activities, professional development, and community engagement, inspiring students to become leaders in technology and innovation.
