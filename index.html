About IEEE SB SSIT

Welcome to the official website of IEEE Student Branch of SSIT. IEEESB-SSIT was Inaugurated in the year 2008. Since then, we have been an active student branch.
We organize various technical workshops, tech-talks, industrial visits and also take part in Humanitarian Work. Our flagship event SAAMARTHYA is an even semester tech fest.
IEEESB-SSIT organizes various technical events to make it's members aware of the advancing technologies and help them know the practical aspects of technologies.
So that it helps the student members to develop themselves apart from the academics. Develop a passion to learn, you will never cease to grow.

Mock Placement

Mock placements are designed to mimic real job interviews or placement exams. Which is conducted by students, Teachers and Placement Co-Ordinators to practice and prepare for actual placement opportunities. It play a vital role in preparing candidates for success in real job interviews or placement exams by providing practice, feedback, and opportunities for skill development and improvement.

IEEE DAY

IEEE Day holds immense significance as a global celebration that brings together the worldwide community of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). Occurring annually on the first Tuesday of October, IEEE Day serves as a platform to commemorate the establishment of IEEE and its contributions to technological advancements.
This day emphasizes the importance of collaboration, knowledge sharing, and networking within the IEEE community. It not only showcases the achievements and innovations of IEEE members but also promotes the role of technology in addressing global challenges. IEEE Day serves as a reminder of the organization's commitment to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity,
fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose among its diverse members.

SAAMARTYA

Samarthya, IEEE SSIT student branch’s flagship event, seamlessly blends knowledge with enjoyment. With stellar speakers, technical events, project exhibitions, paper presentations and fun-filled activities,
it is a dynamic platform that enriches students with the latest industry insights and fosters a vibrant learning environment.
